Corporate Profile

Car Buyer Kenya is a premier exporter and importer of cars from Japan and the United Kingdom. We focus on providing professional solutions that turn complex overseas vehicle procurement into a clear, completely stress-free experience.

For direct sourcing of Japan cars from auction, we maintain a strategic B2B partnership with JCS. This integration guarantees our clients a seamless procurement process, complete vehicle quality control, and fast, transparent services from the auction blocks straight to your destination.

Our Six-Step Buying Process

1

Sourcing From Auction

Identify your target vehicle configurations directly via our extensive auction desk network systems.

2

Place Car Bid / Order

Submit your target vehicle parameters and initiate your structured auction payment or deposit placement as detailed per invoice.

3

Win Bid Confirmation

Upon winning your direct auction bid, clear outstanding Cost and Freight (CnF) balances through verified bank Telegraphic Transfer (TT).

4

Shipment Processing

Your vehicle is immediately fast-tracked into dynamic freight booking queues on the next available vessel route.

5

Custom Clearance

Coordinate documentation structures with your local country clearing agent. We provide complete hands-on assistance for East Africa arrivals.

6

Receive and Own Your Dream Car

Take physical handover keys of your fully documented, certified asset ready for immediate local road use registration.
